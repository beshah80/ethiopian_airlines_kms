# User Guide

## Getting Started

### Accessing the System

1. Navigate to the Ethiopian Airlines Knowledge Portal
2. Enter your email and password
3. Select your preferred language (English or Amharic)
4. Click "Sign In"

**Note**: First-time users may need to complete onboarding to set up their profile.

## Knowledge Base

### Searching for Articles

1. Go to **Knowledge Base** from navigation
2. Enter keywords in the search bar
3. Use filters to narrow results:
   - Department
   - Category (SOP, Best Practice, Safety, etc.)
   - Date range
4. Click on an article to read

### Reading Articles

Articles may contain:
- Rich text with formatting
- Embedded images
- PDF attachments
- Video explanations
- Related articles links

### Acknowledging SOPs

When viewing an SOP:
1. Read the complete procedure
2. Click "Acknowledge" button
3. Your acknowledgment is recorded for compliance

### Creating Articles (Experts/Admins)

1. Click "New Article" button
2. Fill in the form:
   - Title
   - Category
   - Department
   - Content (use rich text editor)
   - Tags for searchability
3. Upload attachments if needed
4. Set status: Draft or Publish
5. Click "Save"

## Expert Locator

### Finding an Expert

1. Go to **Expert Locator**
2. Search by:
   - Expertise keywords (e.g., "737 engines")
   - Department
   - Availability status
3. Browse expert profiles
4. Click "Contact" to initiate inquiry

### Viewing Expert Profiles

Expert profiles show:
- Name and photo
- Areas of expertise
- Years of experience
- Availability status
- Past contributions

### Contacting an Expert

1. Find the expert you need
2. Click on their profile
3. Choose contact method:
   - Send message
   - Request meeting
   - View their articles

### Becoming an Expert

If you have specialized knowledge:
1. Contact your Department Head
2. Request expert status designation
3. Complete your expert profile
4. Start contributing knowledge

## Innovation Hub

### Submitting an Idea

1. Go to **Innovation Hub**
2. Click "Submit New Idea"
3. Fill in the form:
   - Title (clear and concise)
   - Description (problem + proposed solution)
   - Department
   - Estimated impact (Low/Medium/High)
4. Click "Submit"

### Voting on Ideas

1. Browse submitted ideas
2. Click the upvote button on ideas you support
3. View ideas by:
   - Most popular
   - Newest
   - Under review
   - Implemented

### Tracking Your Ideas

View your submitted ideas and their status:
- **Submitted**: Awaiting review
- **Under Review**: Being evaluated
- **Approved**: Accepted for implementation
- **Implemented**: Complete
- **Rejected**: Not moving forward

## Lessons Learned

### Submitting a Lesson Learned

After an incident or significant operation:

1. Go to **Lessons Learned**
2. Click "Submit New Lesson"
3. Complete the form:
   - Incident date
   - Department
   - Summary (what happened)
   - Root cause (why it happened)
   - Corrective action (how we fixed it)
   - Preventability assessment
4. Submit as identified or anonymous

### Searching Lessons Learned

1. Navigate to **Lessons Learned**
2. Use search for specific keywords
3. Filter by:
   - Department
   - Aircraft type
   - Date range
   - Preventability
4. Review similar past incidents

### After-Action Reviews (AAR)

Managers can initiate structured AARs:
1. Create AAR session
2. Invite participants
3. Capture discussion points
4. Generate lesson learned report

## Mobile Usage

### Installing the PWA

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Visit the portal in Chrome
2. Tap menu (⋮)
3. Select "Add to Home screen"
4. Tap "Install"

**iOS (Safari):**
1. Visit the portal in Safari
2. Tap Share button
3. Select "Add to Home Screen"
4. Tap "Add"

### Offline Access

Content you've recently viewed is available offline:
1. Browse articles while online
2. Content is cached automatically
3. Access cached content without internet
4. Changes sync when connection restored

### Low-Bandwidth Mode

When on slow connections:
- Images load progressively
- Videos show thumbnail only
- Text content loads first

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I access the system from home?**
A: Yes, the system is accessible anywhere with internet.

**Q: What if I forget my password?**
A: Click "Forgot Password" on the login page to reset.

**Q: Can I edit someone else's article?**
A: Only your own articles, unless you're an admin or department head.

**Q: How do I become an expert?**
A: Contact your Department Head to request expert designation.

**Q: Is my activity tracked?**
A: Yes, for compliance and to recognize contributions. See your profile for activity history.

**Q: Can I use the system offline?**
A: Yes, content you've viewed is cached. Install the PWA for best offline experience.
