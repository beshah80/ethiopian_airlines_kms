# Ethiopian Airlines Knowledge Management System

## Executive Summary

In the competitive landscape of global aviation, Ethiopian Airlines' primary competitive advantage is its **Institutional Intelligence**. However, as the airline scales toward Vision 2035, it faces a **Fragmentation Crisis**: knowledge is scattered across departments and multiple disconnected portals (SAP, Maintenix, MS Teams).

This project delivers the **Ethiopian Airlines Knowledge Portal (EAKP)**, a unified Knowledge Management System designed to bridge the gap between high-level management strategy and ground-level operational reality. By moving beyond unstructured sharing in Outlook and Telegram, the EAKP institutionalizes the airline's **"Crown Jewels"**—its critical tacit and explicit knowledge assets.

### Expected Impact

| Metric | Current State | Target (6 months) |
|--------|---------------|-------------------|
| Employee Onboarding Time | 6 months | 4 months (33% improvement) |
| Information Retrieval Time | 30+ minutes | < 2 minutes |
| Knowledge System Adoption | N/A | 70% monthly active users |
| Cross-department Collaboration | Minimal | Monthly knowledge sessions |

---

## Project Scope

### In Scope

- **Explicit Knowledge Repository**: Searchable SOPs, manuals, and documentation
- **Expert Locator**: Directory of subject matter experts across all departments
- **Innovation Hub**: Structured idea submission and tracking system
- **Lessons Learned Database**: After-action reviews and incident reporting
- **Mobile-First Design**: Progressive Web App for offline access
- **Multilingual Support**: English and Amharic interfaces

### Out of Scope

- Replacement of existing ERP systems (SAP, Maintenix)
- Flight operations real-time systems integration
- Customer-facing booking systems
